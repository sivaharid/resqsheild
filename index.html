The Mission

⚠ The Problem

Firefighters and rescue personnel operate in extremely hazardous environments with high risks of structural collapse, toxic gas exposure, and sudden incapacitation. Traditional communication often fails in deep structures, leaving teams vulnerable without real-time physiological and environmental telemetry.

⛑ The Solution

A sensor-integrated smart wearable suit designed to autonomously monitor environmental threats and rescuer movements. Utilizing a robust micro-controller and long-range (LoRa) communication, it ensures continuous data relay to command centers, even when standard networks fail.

Core Features

Gas Detection

Detects hazardous levels of LPG, natural gas, smoke, and carbon monoxide in real-time.

MQ-2, MQ-5, MQ-7

Motion & Fall Detection

Continuous monitoring of rescuer orientation to immediately detect unexpected falls or immobility.

MPU6050 (Gyro/Accel)

Location Tracking

Provides real-time location data to coordinate and locate personnel during large-scale emergency operations.

Location Services

LoRa Communication

Transmits vital data over long distances without relying on cellular or Wi-Fi infrastructure.

SX1278 (433MHz)

Local Alert System

Immediate haptic and auditory feedback for the wearer when danger thresholds are breached.

Buzzer, Vibration, LED

Real-time Processing

High-speed data acquisition and edge-processing to minimize response latency.
